Legal Bases

We process personal information on one or more of the following legal bases where applicable:

  • Contract: to provide downloads, licensing, activation, billing, support, renewals, and account-related services.
  • Legitimate interests: to maintain security, prevent fraud and abuse, administer our business relationships, improve the website and product, and defend legal claims.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with tax, accounting, sanctions/export, law-enforcement, and recordkeeping obligations.
  • Consent: where required for non-essential cookies, optional marketing communications, or other processing we ask you to authorize.

How We Share Information

We may share personal information with service providers and other parties in the following categories:

  • Payment / invoicing: to process payments, invoices, receipts, billing records, and tax documentation.
  • Licensing / activation: to issue, validate, transfer, and manage licenses and subscriptions.
  • Hosting / CDN / infrastructure: to host the website, route requests, run forms, secure the site, and maintain availability.
  • Email / support: to receive business purchase requests, send confirmations, and respond to support or legal inquiries.
  • Analytics: none currently configured in the website code.
  • Download distribution: to provide release metadata and downloadable installer assets.
  • Legal / compliance / corporate transaction: where required by law, to protect rights and safety, to investigate misuse, or in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of assets.
