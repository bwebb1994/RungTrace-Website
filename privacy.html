Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RungTrace collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit this website, purchase or activate a license, request support, or use the RungTrace desktop application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, company name, email address, billing details, license requests, support messages, and any files, screenshots, logs, project exports, or diagnostic details you choose to send us.

When you purchase, activate, transfer, or validate a license, we or our licensing providers may process license keys, subscription status, activation dates, product version, device identifiers, machine fingerprints, operating system details, IP address, and related records needed to prevent misuse and provide licensed access.

The RungTrace desktop application is designed to run locally. Unless you choose to send information to us for support or licensing, we do not intentionally collect PLC program files, L5X exports, PLC tag values, controller IP addresses, plant-floor data, or control-system logic from your computer.

Payment And Licensing Providers

Payments, hosted checkout, license activation, subscription administration, and license transfers may be handled by third-party providers such as DevoLens, Cryptolens, payment processors, app hosting services, email providers, and download services. Those providers process information under their own terms and privacy policies. We do not store full payment card numbers on this website.

Website Data

Like most websites, our hosting providers may collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, pages visited, date and time, and error logs. We may use cookies or similar technologies if needed for checkout, security, analytics, or site functionality.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the website and application, process purchases, issue and manage licenses, enable activation and transfers, provide downloads and updates, respond to support requests, troubleshoot problems, improve RungTrace, prevent fraud or abuse, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our agreements.

How We Share Information

We may share information with vendors who help operate the website, process payments, manage licenses, provide downloads, send email, host data, analyze site performance, or provide support. We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights or safety, to investigate misuse, in connection with a business transfer, or with your consent.

Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the product, maintain license and purchase records, support customers, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our agreements. Support files and diagnostic materials you send may be deleted earlier upon request unless we need to retain them for legal, security, or operational reasons.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, especially when information is transmitted over the internet. You are responsible for protecting your own systems, credentials, project files, PLC networks, and backups.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal information where applicable by contacting us through the support link on the RungTrace website. We may need to verify your identity and may retain certain records where required for licensing, accounting, security, legal, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Children

RungTrace is intended for professional and business use. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Use

If you access RungTrace from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our providers operate. Those countries may have different data protection laws than your location.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last-updated date. Continued use of the website or application after an update means you accept the updated policy where permitted by law.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the support link on the RungTrace website.
