Strictly Necessary vs. Optional Technologies

Strictly necessary technologies support core website functions, security, fraud prevention, form handling, checkout, download access, and preference management. These technologies may be required for the website or related services to work.

Analytics, marketing, advertising, or other optional technologies are not required for core site operation. If we add non-essential analytics or marketing technologies, we will ask for consent where required by law before those technologies are used.

Your Choices

If a cookie preferences control is added to the site, you can change consent through that control. Until non-essential cookies are added, there is no website analytics or marketing consent preference to manage on this site.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Browser controls may not affect cookies used by third-party checkout, licensing, or payment providers after you leave the RungTrace website.
