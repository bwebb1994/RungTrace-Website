Subprocessors

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This page lists current subprocessors and service-provider categories used by RungTrace for the website, licensing, support communications, payment or invoicing workflows, and download distribution.

Source note: Processing locations are summarized from each provider's public privacy, DPA, or subprocessor pages reviewed on June 25, 2026. Provider lists can change, so review the linked provider terms during legal review.

Provider Service category Purpose Data categories involved Processing location
Netlify Hosting/CDN/infrastructure Website hosting, serverless form endpoint, security headers, and traffic delivery. Website/device/log data; business purchase request data submitted through hosted functions. United States and other countries where Netlify, its affiliates, and subprocessors operate.
Resend Email delivery Business purchase request notifications and optional confirmations. Contact information and submitted business purchase request content. United States.
Stripe Payment and invoicing Invoices, payment processing, receipts, and payment records. Billing, transaction, and contact information. Various, including United States, Ireland, and other countries listed by Stripe for applicable services.
DevoLens / Cryptolens Licensing and activation Hosted checkout links, license issuance, activation, subscription status, and license management. Contact, licensing, activation, device, and transaction-related records. EEA regions including Ireland, Netherlands, Sweden, Finland, and Germany; limited United States support/chat or third-country access where covered by transfer mechanisms.
GitHub Download distribution Release metadata and downloadable installer assets. Download request metadata and standard service logs. United States and other locations listed by GitHub subprocessors, including Canada, Europe, Australia, Brazil, Japan, Singapore, and other provider regions.
