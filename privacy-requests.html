Privacy Requests

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Use this page to submit privacy rights requests related to RungTrace. You may email the request directly to brady@webbinnovations.com.

Request Types How to Submit Verification Timing Authorized Agents Complaints

Request Types

  • Access to personal information.
  • Correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Deletion of personal information.
  • Restriction of processing.
  • Objection to processing.
  • Portability request.
  • Withdrawal of consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Authorized agent request.

How to Submit

Email brady@webbinnovations.com with the subject line "RungTrace privacy request." Include your name, company, email address, request type, and enough detail for us to locate relevant records.

Do not include passwords, PLC credentials, protected health information, export-controlled information, or other highly sensitive material in your request.

Email a privacy request

Identity Verification

We may need to verify your identity before completing the request. Verification may include confirming control of the email address associated with the request, asking for order or license details, or requesting other information reasonably necessary to match the request to our records.

We may deny or limit requests where permitted by law, including where we cannot verify the request, where another person's rights would be affected, or where retention is required for billing, licensing, security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute handling.

Expected Response Timing

We aim to acknowledge privacy requests promptly and respond within the time required by applicable law. Depending on your location, this may generally be within 30 to 45 days, with extensions where permitted for complex requests.

Authorized Agent Instructions

If an authorized agent submits a request for you, we may require written authorization, proof of the agent's identity, proof of your identity, and direct confirmation that you authorized the request, unless applicable law requires a different process.

Complaint and Escalation Notes

If you are in the EEA or UK, you may have the right to complain to your local data protection supervisory authority. You may also contact us first at brady@webbinnovations.com so we can try to resolve the issue.
